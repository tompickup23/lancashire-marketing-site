# Burnley Social Following Research

Prepared on 17 March 2026.

## Method

I looked for publicly visible follower counts on major platforms and local influencer directories.

Important limitation:

- there does not appear to be a reliable public leaderboard of the top 50 people genuinely from Burnley
- for local creators, the cleanest public directory I found was Modash, but that page is labelled as creators who reach Burnley audiences, not necessarily people from Burnley
- I therefore split the list by confidence

## Bottom line

I could not verify a defensible public top 50 of people from Burnley.

What I can defend as of 17 March 2026 is:

- a top tier of Burnley or Burnley-born public figures led by James Anderson, Briony Raynes, Fabian Coulthard, Cody Frost, and Neil Hodgson
- a second tier of Burnley-local creators and micro-creators visible on public local directories

If a true top 50 is needed, the next step would be a manual profile-by-profile verification pass using official Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and X profile pages, with a stricter rule for what counts as “from Burnley”.
